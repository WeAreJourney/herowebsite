We Are Programmers

Being a Programmer means being in a state of flow, feeling inspiration in every moment of our work. To be truely excellent at what we do, we must have not only a memorization of ideas but a mastery of the mindset behind coding and behind our own emotions. Programmer teaches the most fun parts of programming along with a curriculum on impact, personal evolution, and lifestyle empowering our students to love programming. Innovations such as Code Chunk Libraries make coding accessible for everyone and everything about our course empowers students to move forward and continue to create projects after the course. Vishen Lakhiani

The creator of A-Fest and founder of Mindvalley. Vishen’s book “The Code of the Extraordinary Mind” held the #1 spot on Amazon globally for 5 days in 2017.
